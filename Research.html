GEMINI

The GEMINI (General Medicine Inpatient Initiative) database is the largest network for healthcare data and analytics in Canada, designed to collect detailed clinical patient data and hospital data, which can then be used by researchers in a confidential yet accessible manner. Dr. Fralick is a core GEMINI investigator and helped to develop GEMINI DM, a subset of the GEMINI database that serves as a repository for information on patients living with diabetes. The team frequently uses GEMINI data for their ongoing research studies.
